Strawberry Pancakes with a Cream Cheese Frosting ... 25 Minute Recipe

You will need:
For the Pancakes:
  • 1 Cup of Pancake Mix
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Cup Chopped Strawberries
  • 1 Egg
  • 2/3 Cup Buttermilk
  • 1-2 Tablespoons Coconut Oil
For The Strawberry Cream Cheeze Glaze:
  • 1 Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
  • 3 Ounces Cream Cheese (softened)
  • 1 Cup of Powdered Sugar
  • 2 Tablespoons Milk
Directions:
  1. In a food processor or blender blend the strawberries until they are completely crushed. Scoop out two tablespoons of the strawberry puree and set aside.
  2. In a medium size mixing bowl combine the flour, baking powder, and salt. Add in crushed strawberries, milk, egg and vanilla extract. Combine mixture well.
  3. Heat a large skillet or griddle over medium to medium high heat. Melt one tablespoon of coconut oil. Scoop out the pancake batter with an ice cream scoop.
  4. Cook pancakes for approximately 1-2 minutes until you see little bubbles form, flip pancakes, cook for about one more minute or until golden brown.
  5. Continue until all batter is used, adding oil if you need it, as you go.
  6. For the glaze, combine the powdered sugar, vanilla extract, cream cheese, 2 tablespoons crushed strawberries, 2 tablespoons of milk. Stir well until glaze forms. The glaze can be made by hand, but it is easier with a mixer.
  7. Stack warm pancakes and serve with glaze. Enjoy!
